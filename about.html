a bit about me...

I'm Madi. Madison is a bit too formal for me.

I'm native to perfectly ordinary Lakeland, FL, USA and I think I found what I want to do with my life.

I am currently a 4th-year student in the University of Florida's Digital Arts and Sciences program in the College of Engineering. I am also a musician. I played the French horn with my high school symphony and was in the Fightin' Gator Marching Band for two years.

At UF, I began as a Computer Engineering major, but after taking a 3D Modeling class, found that I was more passionate about the computer graphics aesthetic.

I'm a huge fan of video games, graphics, animation, cinematography and anything Star Wars or Harry Potter. Sometimes though, I do venture outside the Geekdom to play some soccer, take in some college football, cook a little bit, or maybe complete a 5k. When I say that I enjoy 5Ks, I just mean I like running in the non-timed, themed events like the Color Me Rad 5K and the Nerd Run.

So that's a bit about me. Have a fantastic day!

Cheers!
