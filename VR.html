Virtual Reality Experiences

Collaborating with two others, these VR projects were created in Unity over the course of one sememster. Each were made to fulfill a specific goal and more information can be found at the link below each video.


Stranded - A VR Experience

This was the final product of my Senior Project that I completed with three classmates. The idea was to create a narrative, virtual reality experience that educates the user about the symptoms of schizophrenia because mental illness is widely misunderstood and often negatively construed. Because of this, we wanted to make an experience that provides some insight to what living with the disease is really like.
The first video shows one of our group members using the Oculus Rift and going through the experience - my apologizes for the video quality. The second video demonstrates some of the specifics that I worked on : character movement, safe interaction, puzzle development.
