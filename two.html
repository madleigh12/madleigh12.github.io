2D Portfolio

This contains logos and designs created in Illustrator, Photoshop and Inkscape. Some were made for classes, others for special causes and still more just for fun.

Employer Relations Ambassadors

As the Design Chair for ERA, I was tasked with creating a new logo to represent the club. Because of the professional nature of the club, I tried to create a refined and simple logo. I used a diamond motif because ERA's umbrella organization uses that shape in most of their graphics.

UF TYPE

UF TYPE is a new outreach program affiliated with the UF Computer and Information Science and Engineering Department. We work with Girls Who Code to promote computer science to middle-school and high-school students.

The Purple Beast

The Purple Beast is a mountain biking/ trail running fundraiser put on by the Pancreatic Cancer Alliance of Polk County, Inc., a non-profit organization raising money for pancreatic cancer research.

Relay for Life

These two logos were made for a Relay for Life team benefiting the American Cancer Society. Their team name was Commotion in Motion and their theme was Spider-Man.

Rotoscopes
*I do not own any of the original basis of any images referencing a movie, video game, musical or series.*

I discovered rotoscoping when I took a Photoshop class and, it appealed to me so much, that I started doing it in my spare time. It may be because I would doodle mazes of irregular polygons in my notebok, but I can't tell you exactly why this art style appeals to me; maybe because it's minimalist, maybe because it's a bottom-up style. But, as you can probably tell, it does allow me to express some of my interests.
